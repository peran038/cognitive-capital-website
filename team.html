Our Team

Dr. Evelyn Reed

Founder and Managing Partner

Dr. Evelyn Reed is the Founder and Managing Partner of Cognitive Capital. She brings a rare, dual perspective to deep technology investing, combining a rigorous, first-principles background in artificial intelligence research with a two-decade track record of successful venture capital investing. Her career has been defined by a singular focus: identifying and nurturing the foundational technologies that create new markets and redefine existing ones.

Evelyn's journey into technology began at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), where she earned her PhD in Computational Neuroscience. Her research focused on developing novel neural network architectures inspired by the human brain's ability to learn from sparse, incomplete data. This work, which was published in several leading academic journals, provided her with a deep, technical understanding of the principles of intelligence, both biological and artificial. Following her PhD, she joined a major technology research lab as a lead AI researcher, where she was instrumental in developing some of the earliest large-scale machine learning systems for real-world applications.

Transitioning to venture capital 20 years ago, Evelyn quickly established a reputation for her ability to look beyond surface-level trends and perform deep, technical diligence. She has led early-stage investments in multiple category-defining companies in enterprise software, semiconductors, and applied AI, resulting in several multi-billion dollar exits.

Her investment philosophy, which forms the bedrock of Cognitive Capital, is rooted in her research background. It is this thesis-driven, analytical approach that allows Cognitive Capital to identify the generational companies that are not just participating in the AI revolution, but are architecting its very foundations.

Adrian Perez

Founding Engineer

Adrian Perez is a Founding Engineer at Cognitive Capital, where he is responsible for building the firm's internal AI-powered investment analysis platform. He has a deep background in building and scaling data-intensive applications, with a focus on machine learning and natural language processing.

Prior to joining Cognitive Capital, Adrian was a Senior Software Engineer at a leading fintech company, where he developed and deployed machine learning models for fraud detection and risk assessment. He holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from Stanford University, where his research focused on the application of deep learning to financial data.
